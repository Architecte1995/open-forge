# 🪉 The Open Forge

&gt; *Where numbers meet stories, and owls fall from nests at 4:19 in the morning.*

---

## What is this?

This is not a startup. This is not a product. This is a **digital curiosity cabinet** built by a sleep-deprived owl and his Dragon-Genie accomplice.

The Open Forge is three things in a trenchcoat pretending to be a website:

1. **An aesthetic coquetry** — A place where serif fonts meet starfields, where night mode is the default, and where a red triangle wobbles at you if you dare switch to daylight.
2. **A digital library** — Our games, tools, and mathematical oddities, hosted on Cloudflare Workers because Netlify links have a habit of dying like forgotten palindromes.
3. **A fable** — Read the story of Merlin. It explains everything (and nothing).
